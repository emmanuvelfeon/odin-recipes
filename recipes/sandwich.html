Tomato Basil Sandwich

Tomato Basil Sandwich is made with the goodness of tomatoes, garlic, cheese and mild spices. This delish recipe is super simple and can be made in just a few minutes without spending much time in the kitchen. So, try it today and enjoy a delicious treat for breakfast or as a snack.

Ingredients

  • 6 slices bread- brown
  • 9 leaves sweet basil
  • black pepper as required
  • 1 tablespoon oregano
  • 1 tablespoon garlic
  • 3 tomato
  • salt as required
  • 1 teaspoon chilli flakes
  • 1 cup low fat mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. To begin with this easy recipe, wash and clean the tomatoes and basil leaves. Chop the tomatoes.
  2. Next, take the bread slices and layer them with butter. In the meantime, take a large bowl and add tomatoes, 1 tablespoon of minced garlic, salt, pepper, chili flakes, oregano and grated 1 cup mozzarella cheese. Toss it well and add basil leaves.
  3. Take the bread slices and add the stuffing and grill it for 5 to 7 minutes. Serve hot with dip or ketchup and enjoy!
